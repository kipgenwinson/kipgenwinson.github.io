Spent the week with three farming families who are moving their first plots from poppy to **coffee and cardamom** under ALSIPOP. What struck me most wasn't the crop plan — it was how ready they were. The hills here have always grown things well; the question was only ever markets and dignity.

We mapped shade trees, agreed on a chemical-free protocol, and set a first harvest target. Slow work, done family by family.
